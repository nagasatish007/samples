# Escalation Guide

Escalate or recommend follow-up when:

- The device will not power on after basic troubleshooting.
- The customer reports heat, smoke, burning smell, or battery swelling.
- Physical damage is involved.
- Data-loss risk is present.
- The troubleshooting steps do not resolve the issue after the first pass.

For non-safety issues, prefer giving the customer a short checklist before escalation.
